1 Who We Are

GalliExpress is a hyperlocal delivery platform serving Addanki, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh, India. We operate the GalliExpress Customer App, Partner App, and Rider App (collectively, "the Service").

When this policy says "GalliExpress," "we," "us," or "our," it means the team operating the GalliExpress platform.

Contact: support@galliexpress.in

3 How We Use Your Information

  • Fulfil your orders — share your delivery address and order details with the shop and assigned rider
  • Send order updates — push notifications, WhatsApp messages about your order status
  • Process payments — pass payment data securely to Razorpay; we never store card or UPI credentials
  • Run GalliCoins — award and track loyalty coins based on your orders
  • Provide support — respond to your queries and resolve disputes
  • Improve the Service — analyse usage patterns to make the app better
  • Prevent fraud — detect and block abusive or fraudulent activity

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising to non-GalliExpress entities.

6 Your Rights

Under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information — via your in-app profile settings
  • Delete your account — use "Delete Account" in Settings, or email us
  • Withdraw consent for optional data uses (e.g., promotional notifications) via app settings
  • Lodge a grievance — email support@galliexpress.in; we will respond within 30 days
Deleting your account removes your profile, saved addresses, and GalliCoins. Order history is retained for 3 years as required by applicable law.

7 Children's Privacy

GalliExpress is not intended for use by anyone under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8 Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Firebase Authentication with OTP — no passwords stored
  • Firestore security rules limiting data access to the relevant parties only
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Razorpay PCI-DSS compliant payment processing

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to support@galliexpress.in.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via a push notification or a banner in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect when the policy was last revised.

Continuing to use GalliExpress after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
