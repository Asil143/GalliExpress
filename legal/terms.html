2 What GalliExpress Does

GalliExpress is a technology platform that connects:

  • Customers — who want to order food and groceries from local shops in Addanki
  • Shop Partners — local restaurants, tiffin centres, and grocery shops who list their products
  • Delivery Riders — independent contractors who fulfil deliveries

GalliExpress is a technology intermediary, not a restaurant or logistics company. The contract for the sale of food/goods is between the Customer and the Shop Partner. The delivery contract is between the Customer and the Rider. GalliExpress facilitates these transactions but is not a party to them.

5 Orders, Payments and Cancellations

Payments

GalliExpress accepts the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) — pay the rider at your doorstep
  • UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • Digital Wallets (Paytm Wallet, Amazon Pay, etc.)

Online payments are processed by Razorpay. By paying online, you also agree to Razorpay's Terms of Service. GalliExpress does not store your card or UPI credentials.

Pricing

Item prices are set by Shop Partners and may change without prior notice. The price shown at the time of placing an order is the price you pay. A delivery fee and 5% GST applies to most orders.

Cancellation

When you cancelOutcome
Within 60 seconds of placing the orderFull refund (if prepaid)
After shop has accepted / started preparingNo refund — food preparation has begun

Refunds

Refunds are issued in these circumstances:

  • You received the wrong item
  • Your order was not delivered
  • Items were significantly damaged or different from what was described
  • The shop cancelled your order

Refund requests must be raised within 24 hours of the scheduled delivery time through the app or by contacting support. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Refunds are at GalliExpress's discretion for COD orders where payment has already been made. Partial refunds may be issued where appropriate.

By registering as a Shop Partner, you agree to:

  • Hold a valid FSSAI licence for food businesses
  • Accept or reject incoming orders in a timely manner (within 5 minutes)
  • Prepare orders to the standard described in your menu

GalliExpress charges a platform commission on each completed order. The commission rate applicable to your account is communicated separately during onboarding. Payouts for completed orders are processed every Monday for the prior week's deliveries, to the bank account or UPI ID you provide.

Suspension

GalliExpress may suspend or remove a shop from the platform if the shop receives consistently poor ratings, fails to fulfil orders reliably, violates food safety laws, or engages in fraudulent activity.

Riders are independent contractors, not employees of GalliExpress. By registering as a Rider, you agree that:

  • You are at least 18 years old and hold a valid Indian driving licence appropriate for your vehicle
  • Your vehicle is roadworthy, insured, and registered in your name or with your permission

  • You will handle food orders with care and deliver within the estimated time
  • You will take a photo proof of delivery at the customer's doorstep
  • You will treat customers, shop staff, and GalliExpress support staff with respect

Rider Earnings

Riders earn a fixed delivery fee per completed order. The applicable fee is communicated in the Rider App. Earnings for each week are paid every Monday to the bank account or UPI ID you provide in the app.

Tips

Customers may add an optional delivery tip. 100% of any tip goes to the assigned rider.

Deactivation

GalliExpress may deactivate a rider account for: poor delivery ratings, failure to complete accepted orders, rude behaviour, traffic violations reported by customers, or fraudulent activity.
